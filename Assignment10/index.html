Games you should try

There are many games out there that have changed the gaming world as of 2024. Some surpassing even the tripple A companies. This Page is a memorial for the experiences that revolutionized gaming!

Helldivers 2
Helldivers 2 is a third-person shooter set in a galactic war. You play as a helldiver to fight for freedom! There are many enemies, and many ways to stomp them. You fight along as time shapes the galactic war!

Lethal Company
This game is a co-op horror game about scavenging for scrap to sell to the Company. You have a quota to meet, and if you dont meet that quota... Lets not talk about it. Monsters await in the derilict halls, most of them ready to snap your spine, providing a challenge. Happy collecting!

Palworld
Palworld is when Pokemon and Ark had a baby. You fight, build, and work alongside "Pals" with unique abilities, ranging from resource collecting, to devastating miniguns!
